About

Make Minecraft somewhat enjoyable.

For Vanilla Players

We strive to make an enhanced vanilla experience for those that don't want to read through pages after pages of help commands. For those that just want to play Minecraft, we provide the chance to experience a harder experience through diseases and thirst, while also keeping the default Minecraft progression alive.

For Players That Want More

We have many plugins designed to change the Minecraft experience. Whether you are just trying to explore features of your favourite fandom in Minecraft or trying to create the most powerful civilisation on the server, there will always be something for you to do. Since we also cater to players that want a more vanilla experience, these additional features are designed to ease into, rather than forcing you through a lengthy tutorial to explain everything!

Our Features

Check out some of the features we add to the Minecraft experience!

Economy

Earn money by just playing, trade with other players, and grow your empire.

Civilisations

Group up with your friends to create metropolises, and go to war with all those who oppose you.

Diseases

Fight nature, as well as other players. Discover vaccines and medicine to combat the many diseases on the server.

Level Grinds

Everyone loves an MMO grind. Level up in your jobs to earn more money, or train your stats to be the strongest player.

PVP

Go to war in groups or fight solo in the wilderness. The choice is yours.

Exploration

Hop in a TARDIS or use the ship port to explore worlds from multiple fandoms.
